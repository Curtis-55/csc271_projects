How do I create an account?

To create an account, download the ModMarket app from the App Store or Google Play, then follow the on-screen instructions to sign up using your email address or social media accounts.

Is there a fee to use ModMarket?

ModMarket offers a free basic account with limited features. A Pro subscription is available for users who want access to advanced features and a Seller Account for those looking to buy and sell parts.

How do I track my modifications?

Once logged in, navigate to the "Mod Tracking" section of the app. Here, you can add details about each modification, including parts used, costs, and installation dates.

How do I buy or sell parts?

To buy or sell parts, go to the "Marketplace" section of the app. You can browse listings, filter by compatibility and location, and contact sellers directly through the app. To list an item for sale, simply create a new listing with photos and details about the part.
