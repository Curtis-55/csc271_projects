Steak Frites

Steak Frites is my favorite dish becuase it is the perfect blend of flavors. When cooked perfectly creating a comforting yet indulgent meal. A special memory I have had with this dish is ordering it at the River Bar in westerly. When it was served it had very delicous crispy golden fries and the steak was very tender with lots of flavor as it was cooked in a red wine sauce.

Ingredients for Steak and Frites Quantity
salt / pepper 1 tsp of each
garlic 1x clove
butter 2 tbsp
thyme/ rosemary 1x tsp
oil 1x tbsp
potatoes 3x potatos
ribeye 1x
